About the Artist

A Voice from the Highlands

Jaytham's work explores the intersection of urban life and ancestral memory. With over a decade of performance experience across East Africa and Europe, his pieces are crafted to be both intensely personal and universally accessible.

He believes in the power of the 'digital hearth'—spaces where technology amplifies human connection rather than diluting it. Every performance is an invitation to gather, listen, and reflect.
